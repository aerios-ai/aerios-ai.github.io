Flight¶

A flight is one of the main building blocks in Aerios. A flight is defined by an aircraft taking a uni-directional journey from one airport to another.

class aerios.flight.Flight(airports, aircraft_type=None, aircraft_registration=None)[source]¶

Definition of a flight.

Parameters

  • airports (list) – A list of two airports, defined as strings by their IATA codes.

  • aircraft_type (string) – The type of the aircraft, i.e., boeing-747-400, or airbus a380.

  • aircraft_registration (string) – The aircraft registration, i.e., A6-EDB. Note that this is not the manufacturer’s ID.
